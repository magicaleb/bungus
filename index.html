ğŸ² What is D&D?

Dungeons & Dragons is a collaborative storytelling game where you and your friends create adventures together. One person is the Dungeon Master (DM) who guides the story, while others play characters in that story.

ğŸ¯ Common Actions

Ability Check: Roll d20 + your ability modifier to try something
Attack Roll: Roll d20 + attack bonus to hit enemies
Damage Roll: Roll weapon dice to determine damage dealt
Saving Throw: Roll d20 + saving throw modifier to resist effects

DM (Dungeon Master): The storyteller and referee
Initiative: Roll d20 to determine turn order in combat
